Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use World Cup 2026 Fan Hub.

Effective date: April 20, 2026

Overview

World Cup 2026 Fan Hub is built to provide match schedules, scores, predictions, and account-related app features with limited data collection. Most app preferences are stored locally on your device.

Information We Handle

  • Name, favorite team, app preferences, and predictions saved on your device.
  • Purchase and restoration status related to the Pro upgrade through Google Play.
  • Technical request information required to load fixtures, venues, and live score updates from sports data providers.
  • Notification permission status if you enable reminders on your device.

How Information Is Used

  • To show match schedules, live scores, venues, and standings.
  • To save your predictions and profile preferences on your device.
  • To unlock and restore Pro features you purchase.
  • To improve reliability, fix issues, and maintain the app experience.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Billing for Pro purchases and purchase restoration.
  • Football data providers to fetch fixtures, results, and match updates.
  • Android and Flutter platform services for notifications and connectivity support.

These services may process limited technical data under their own terms and privacy policies.

Data Storage

Predictions, profile details, and cached app content are primarily stored locally on your device. We do not sell personal information.

Permissions and Choices

  • You can edit or remove profile information inside the app.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored data from your device.
  • You can manage notification permissions in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

World Cup 2026 Fan Hub is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe data has been provided by a child in error, please contact us.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or service updates. The latest version should be considered the current policy.
